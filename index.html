Innovations for a Better Tomorrow

IBeTo (Innovations for a Better Tomorrow) is the flagship event of Excel. It is a search for ideas and innovators capable of aiding social upliftment. IBETO was born out of the conviction that technology has the answers to some of societyâ€™s most daunting challenges. This national level pursuit has received overwhelming responses from all over India and have brought several innovations to the forefront. The brightest of these ideas are recognized and mentored to realize the end product. IBeTo, thus, strives to be the bridge joining social relevance and technical innovation, to connect the pioneer and the populace.

HACKATHON

The IBeTo Hackathon is being held so as to get a working prototype based on the problem statements. These prototypes can further be iterated and be launched into the market. In addition to the prize money, the top 3 contestants will get a direct entry into the IBeTo finals which guarantees them proper mentorship and guidance from the experts in their field.

Duration: 24 hours

Prizes worth 15k

Venue: Kerala Startup Mission, North Kalamassery, Kochi

Problem Statements

IBeTo 2017 will curate and foster ideas that adhere to the following problem statements and ultimately usher in a better tomorrow:

  • Revolutionizing Road Safety

  • Innovations beneficial for the Differently abled

  • Providing easy educational access for all

  • Betterment of General Health care in Rural Areas

  • Combating environmental degradation while ensuring energy conservation
